# 💡 intro1 — Hints

## Step 1
Bash recognizes command names **exactly**. `eko` is not a command. There is only one correct name that looks similar.

## Step 2
The correct name has 4 letters, starts with `e` and ends with `o`: **e _ _ o**

## Step 3
It is the `echo` command (prints text to stdout). Covered in **section 1.5** of the book.
